# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** November 16, 2025

## 1. Introduction

PartyPilot ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our event planning application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Email address, name, phone number, password (encrypted)
- **Trip Information:** Event details, dates, locations, group size, preferences
- **Usage Data:** IP address, browser type, device information, access times

### Automatically Collected Information
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies
- Log data (pages viewed, time spent, clicks)
- Device and browser information

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain our service
- Create and manage your trips
- Send notifications about your events
- Communicate with you about your account
- Improve our services
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:
- **Venue Partners:** Booking providers (OpenTable, Resy, etc.) when making reservations
- **AI Service Providers:** OpenAI for trip planning (prompt data only)
- **Analytics Providers:** Error tracking and monitoring (Sentry)
- **Legal Authorities:** When required by law

We DO NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your data (GET /api/users/export)
- **Deletion:** Request account and data deletion (DELETE /api/users/account)
- **Rectification:** Update your information
- **Portability:** Export your data in JSON format
- **Withdraw Consent:** Opt out of communications

**To exercise your rights:** Email privacy@partypilot.app or use the in-app data export/deletion features.

## 6. Data Retention

- Active account data: Retained while your account is active
- Deleted accounts: Personal data anonymized immediately, trip data retained for 30 days
- Backups: Retained for 90 days

## 7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Password encryption (bcrypt with 12 rounds)
- HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit
- JWT authentication
- Regular security audits
- Database access controls

## 8. Children's Privacy

PartyPilot is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe we have collected such data, contact us immediately.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure adequate safeguards are in place.

## 10. Cookies

We use cookies for:
- Authentication (essential)
- Analytics (optional - can be disabled)
- Preferences (optional)

You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

## 12. Contact Us

For privacy questions or concerns:
- **Email:** privacy@partypilot.app
- **Data Protection Officer:** dpo@partypilot.app

## 13. Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process your data based on:
- **Consent:** When you create an account
- **Contract Performance:** To provide our services
- **Legitimate Interests:** Service improvement and security
- **Legal Obligations:** Compliance with laws
